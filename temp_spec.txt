1. Introduction & ScopeThis document defines the complete functional and high-level technical design of the AKS DigiRec Ceramics Factory Management System, from Home Page to Production, Costing and User Management. It is written so that a MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) implementation team can directly convert it into backend APIs and frontend screens with clear field definitions, formulas, integrations, and flows.
Modules covered: Home & Authentication, Dashboard, Master Data (Sections, Raw Materials, Suppliers, Workers, Customers, Finished Goods), Workers Activity, Composition Manager & Ball Mills, Production, Daily Cash Register, Cash Bank, Contacts & Directory, Word Dictionary, User Management, Overheads & Costing, Reports & Printing, and System Architecture (MERN).
2. System Architecture Overview (MERN)AKS DigiRec is implemented as a multi-tenant SaaS-style MERN application:• Backend: Node.js + Express + MongoDB (Mongoose) – RESTful JSON APIs.• Frontend: React SPA (with React Router and Axios) – all screens, forms, grids, and dashboards.• Multi-tenant: each company (Admin) has its own data identified by companyId; Super Admin can manage all companies; operators see only their company’s data based on permissions.• Authentication: JWT-based; roles: SUPER_ADMIN, ADMIN, OPERATOR.
Core backend modules:• auth – login, signup, OTP verification, trial periods.• users – Super Admin, Admin, Operator management and permissions.• master – Sections, Raw Materials, Suppliers, Workers, Customers, Finished Goods, Word Dictionary.• workers-activity – daily workers activity, Close Day, Close Week.• composition – Ball Mills, compositions, processed stock (Clay/Glaze/Color).• production – production stages, WIP and losses, movement into Finished Goods.• cash-register – Sales, Purchases, Sales Returns, Purchase Returns, Cash, Bank, Expenses.• bank – bank accounts, fund transfers, cheque tracking.• contacts – phone & contacts directory.• reports – dashboards, ledgers, stock reports, income & expenditure, model cost sheet.• logs – audit logs of critical changes.
3. Roles, Tenancy & SecurityUser roles:1) Super Admin – full access across all companies; can create Admins, extend trials, freeze/slow accounts, send system messages, and control permissions.2) Admin – owner of a company; can manage own data, create exactly one Operator, control Operator permissions, and manage their company profile and word dictionary.3) Operator – works inside a single company’s data; can enter and view data based on Admin-approved permissions; cannot manage users and has no cross-company access.
Tenancy rules:• Each company has a record in companies collection (name, logo, contacts, settings).• All master and transactional data includes companyId.• Super Admin can query by any companyId; Admin and Operator are limited to their own companyId.• Each company acts like its own directory; changes by one company cannot affect another.
Trial & licensing:• New Admin accounts have a 3-month trial by default.• Title bar shows: 'Trial Period – expires in XX days'.• After trial expiry, system shows a forced message every hour for ~15 seconds, blocking work, instructing user to contact Super Admin by phone and email for registration.• Super Admin can extend trial, fully register, or slow/freeze any account.
4. Global Conventions
4.1 Auto-Code GenerationEvery master entity uses structured auto codes:• Sections: SEC-###-Name (e.g. SEC-001-Molding)• Raw Materials: MAT-###-Name• Suppliers: SUP-###-Name• Workers: WRK-###-Name• Customers: CUST-###-Name• Finished Goods: FG-###-Name• Production Batches: PROD-###• Invoices: INV-###
4.2 Phone & City Fields• Phone numbers use an international country code dropdown with flags; default is Pakistan (+92).• For Pakistan: format +92XXXXXXXXXX with fixed 10 digits after +
92.• For other countries: 10 to 13 digits allowed (after country code).• City fields use a shared city list with 'Add More'; new cities are stored for all future use.
4.3 Color Integration System• Finished Goods have a Color field (text + optional color picker).• Production and stock track color variants separately.• Sales deduct stock by specific color.• Inventory maintains min/max per color if needed.• Dashboard and reports can analyse sales by color popularity.
Universal status colors:• 🔴 Red – Critical / attention needed (low stock, overdue, credit limit exceeded).• 🟡 Yellow – Warning / medium priority.• 🟢 Green – Normal / OK.• 🔵 Blue – Information / max level.• ⚫ Gray – Inactive / disabled.• 🟠 Orange – Special / rare category items.
4.4 Printing & ExportAll tables and reports support printing and export: JPG/PDF. Options:• Period selection: Current Day, Current Week, Current Month, Custom Date Range.• Group-wise (e.g. raw materials by type, sections by group) or all groups together.• Default paper size: A4; additional sizes including small/thermal printers selectable.
Header (centered above report body):1) Company Name (from logged-in Admin’s profile).2) Second line: Report Name.3) Third line: Period (From – To) and current date/time of printing.
Footer (right-aligned on every page):• AKS_DigiRec (with logo),• Cell No.: +92 300 6238557,• Email: aksdigirec@gmail.com.
4.5 Word Dictionary (Bilingual Names)• Word Dictionary stores mapping of default labels to Urdu and user-customized labels.• For each label (tab, sub tab, field, column):  - Default English label (key – immutable system-wide).  - Default Urdu label.  - Custom English label (per company).  - Custom Urdu label (per company).• Users can right-click a label and edit its appearance in English/Urdu; this changes only the view for that company, not the internal keys.• Dictionary changes are applied across UI, including tabs, sub tabs and fields, without affecting other companies.

6. DashboardDashboard is the main internal landing page after login.
Layout:• Top: Title bar with logo, company name, logged-in user name, role, trial/registration status.• Center: 8 main tabs displayed as horizontal navigation (Master Data, Workers Activity, Daily Cash Register, Cash Bank,   Composition Manager, Production, Contacts & Directory, User Management).• Left panel: 'Today’s Activities' ticker – recent actions, alerts, closing day/week reminders.• Right panel: 'Warnings & Alerts' – low stock, overdue payments, credit limit warnings, trial expiry, etc.• Summary metrics: Total Sales Today, Cash Balance, Bank Balance, Processed Stocks (Clay/Glaze/Color), Finished Goods summary,   Workers pending payments.

7. Master Data TabMaster Data holds all static and semi-static reference information used across the system. It includes Sections, Raw Materials, Suppliers, Workers, Customers, Finished Goods, and (optionally) Bank structures.
7.1 Sections Sub-TabPurpose: Define Section Groups, Main Sections, and Sub Sections for production and workers activity, including Non Material sections that only record labour.
Fields:• Section Group (dropdown + Add More): Clay Group, Glaze & Color Group, Kiln Group, Flower Group, Sticker Group,   Packing Group, Other.• Main Section (text/dropdown + Add More): e.g. Clay Forming, Glaze Coating, Bisque Kiln.• Sub Section Name (text): e.g. Handle Making, Cup Body, Kiln Loading.• Auto Code: SEC-###-Name (auto-generated).• Non Material (Labor Only) – checkbox: when checked, no material is deducted for this sub section; only labour cost is recorded.• Description (text area).• Status – Active / Inactive.
Table columns:• Serial No.• Section Group• Main Section• Sub Section Name• Code• Non Material (Yes/No)• Description• Status (Active/Inactive)• Actions (CRUD with password confirmation; changes logged).
7.2 Raw Materials Sub-TabPurpose: Define raw materials and special materials (Dyeing, Mould), track stock, amounts, and min/max levels.
Fields:• Material Type (dropdown with Add More): Clay Material, Glaze Material, Color Material, Flower Material, Sticker Material,   Packing Material, Dyeing Material, Mould Material, Other.• Material Name (text input) – or Model Name when type is Dyeing/Mould (linked to Finished Goods model).• Auto Code: MAT-###-Name (auto).• Unit of Measurement (dropdown + Add More): Kg, Liters, Pieces, Bags.• Stock (number) – current quantity at first entry treated as opening stock.• Amount (number) – total monetary value entered manually.• Rate (auto) – average rate = Amount / Stock (if stock > 0).• Min Stock (number).• Max Stock (number).• Opening Balance Date (optional date): if not provided, saved date is used.• Details (optional text).
Additional fields for Flower/Sticker/Packing types:• Pieces per Sheet / Pack (number).• Pieces Remaining in Current Sheet (system-managed).• Model Binding (optional) – preferred model or size these materials are used for (for reporting).
Special logic for Dyeing & Mould Material:• Dyeing Material Life = 5000 pieces (configurable).• Mould Material Life = 160 pieces (configurable).• Material Name selected from Finished Goods models; system tracks how many pieces have been produced using that   dye/mould, allocating cost per piece.• When life is consumed, remaining value can be:  - Disposed as zero-price sale, or  - Transferred to an expense category (e.g. 'Mould Disposal') via Cash Register.
Table columns:• Code• Material Type• Material Name• Unit• Stock• Rate (auto-average)• Amount• Min Stock• Max Stock• Status (color dot: Red – ≤ min, Green – between, Blue – ≥ max)• Actions (CRUD, Right-click).
Right-click options:• View Details.• Edit Material.• Sell via Daily Cash Register – opens Sales screen with Item Type = Raw Material, this item selected.• Generate Ledger/Report – material stock movement and valuation.• Print/Export (JPG/PDF).• Send WhatsApp (share material details).• Delete (with restrictions / only when safe).
7.3 Suppliers Sub-TabPurpose: Manage suppliers of raw materials, equipment, and services.
Fields:• Supplier Type (dropdown): Raw Material, Equipment, Service, Other.• Supplier Name.• Auto Code: SUP-###-Name.• Contact Person.• Cell Number (with country code & validation).• Email (validated).• Address.• City (dropdown + Add More, default Gujrat if desired).• Payment Terms (dropdown + Add More): Cash, 7 days, 15 days, 30 days, Custom Days.• Opening Balance (number) – initial payable.• Default Material Type (optional): when creating purchases, can auto-filter materials.• Default Material (optional): quick ordering of a frequent material.
Table columns:• Code• Supplier Name• Contact Person• Cell No.• Balance (current payable)• Payment Terms• Status (Green = Active, Gray = Inactive, Red = Blacklisted)• Days to Pay (auto: from purchase date + payment terms)• ActionsRight-click options:• Create Purchase Order / Purchase Invoice – opens Daily Cash Register with Supplier pre-selected.• View Supplier Ledger – all transactions, payable analysis.• Print/Export Supplier Statement.• Send WhatsApp / Email.• Blacklist / Activate.• Delete (only if no transactions).
7.4 Workers Sub-TabPurpose: Maintain workers master records, including fixed advances and section assignment.
Fields:• Worker Name.• Auto Code: WRK-###-Name.• Advance (Fixed) – long-term loan/advance amount; adjusted via Daily Cash Register.• Section Group (from Sections).• Main Section.• Sub Section.• Worker Type (dropdown + Add More): Per Piece, Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Office, Temporary, Other.• Join Date.• Father Name.• CNIC (13-digit format: 12345-6789012-3).• Cell Number.• Address.• City.• Emergency Contact.• Referred By.• Status: Active / Inactive.• Notes (optional).
Table columns:• Code• Worker Name• Father Name• CNIC• Worker Type• Section (Group/Main/Sub)• Advance (Fixed)• Emergency Contact• Referred By• Status (toggle – Active/Inactive; Active sorted first)• Actions (CRUD).
Right-click options:• View Details – show complete worker profile.• Edit Worker.• Add/Edit Advance (Fixed) – opens Cash Register with worker pre-selected; updates both worker Advance (Fixed) and cash.• View Attendance – history from Workers Activity.• Generate Report – worker ledger (worked amount, payments, advance used, total payable).• Record Work Today – jumps to Workers Activity tab pre-filtered for this worker.• Send WhatsApp / Call.• Delete (only if safe).
All CRUD operations can be password-protected by Admin; changes by Operator are logged and shown in Dashboard alerts.
7.5 Customers Sub-TabPurpose: Manage customers for finished goods and raw material sales.
Fields:• Customer Name.• Auto Code: CUST-###-Name.• Customer Type (dropdown + Add More): Retail, Wholesale, Corporate, Export, Other.• Contact Person.• Cell Number.• Email.• Address.• City.• Credit Limit (number).• Payment Terms (dropdown + Add More): Cash, 7/15/30 days, Custom.• Opening Balance (number) – receivable.• Status: Active / Inactive / Blacklisted.
Table columns:• Code• Customer Name• Type• Contact Person• Cell No.• Balance (current receivable)• Credit Limit• Payment Terms• Status (Green Active, Gray Inactive, Red Blacklisted)• Days to Pay (overdue/remaining vs terms)• ActionsCredit limit logic:• When generating a Sale Invoice, system checks: current balance + invoice amount vs credit limit.• If ≥ limit: system shows a clear warning (Red) but does not block invoice creation.• Warnings are logged and can be seen in dashboard alerts.
7.6 Finished Goods Sub-TabPurpose: Define all finished products (models) with sizes, packing and material usage data for costing.
Fields:• Product Name.• Auto Code: FG-###-Name.• Size (dropdown + Add More): 10, 8, 6, 5, Bora (1x1).• Unit (dropdown + Add More): Boxes, Nos., Bora.• Category (dropdown + Add More): Simple, Color, Full Color, Flower, Full Flower, Other.• Color (text + optional color picker).• Gross Weight (grams per piece) – for Clay consumption.• Gross Glaze (grams per piece) – for Glaze consumption.• Gross Color (grams per piece) – for Color consumption.• Cost Price (per piece or per box) – can be auto-calculated but also editable by Admin (optional input).• Selling Price (per piece or per box).• Stock (current quantity; system-managed from production & sales).• Min Stock.• Max Stock.• Status.
Table columns:• Code• Product Name• Size• Category• Color• Gross Weight (g)• Gross Glaze (g)• Gross Color (g)• Unit• Current Stock• Min Stock• Max Stock• Cost Price (Admin-visible only unless allowed)• Selling Price• Status (stock status Red/Green/Blue)• Item Type (Regular/Average/Rare based on turnover)• Sale Status (Available/Sold-out flags)• Actions.
Right-click options:• View Details.• Edit Product.• Record Production – opens Production/Workers Activity with this model selected.• Generate Report – production, sale, stock and profit analysis for this model.• Sell via Daily Cash Register – opens Sales with item pre-selected.• Send WhatsApp (e.g. model image and details to customer).• Delete (restricted to safe conditions).
8. Workers Activity TabPurpose: Daily/weekly record of each worker’s attendance, work, payment, and section-wise production; links labour to production and costing.
Layout:• Date selector with current date and Urdu day name.• Navigation buttons: Previous Day / Next Day.• Buttons: 'Close Day' (every day) and 'Close Week' (visible on Thursday).• Section-wise tables: each section group/main/sub has its own table showing active workers only.
Table columns per section:• Worker – name (from Workers).• Attendance – Present/Absent toggle (if absent, row disabled for work entry).• Balance B/F – brought-forward from previous day's Balance C/F.• Worked Amount – total from popup (read-only).• Advance (Daily) – manual input for cash given today; auto-posted to Cash Register and worker ledger.• Amount to Pay – auto = Balance B/F + Worked Amount – Advance (Daily).• Paid – manual input (cash paid today); auto-posted to Cash Register.• Balance C/F – auto = Amount to Pay – Paid, carried to next day.• Section / Sub Section (for reference).• Actions (open popup, ledger).
Worked Amount popup:• Model Name (mandatory dropdown from Finished Goods; 'Add New Model' opens mini Finished Goods form and saves model).• Category (from Finished Goods; optional view).• Color (optional; required if 'Color Required' toggle is on for that model).• Qty Worked (number).• Rate (number) – per piece.• Amount (auto: Qty × Rate).• Additional Labor (manual amount – extras).• Net Amount (auto: sum of amounts + additional labor).
Color Required logic:• Each model can have a 'Color Required' toggle defined in Finished Goods or at popup row.• If on, then in Glaze/Color sections, color must be selected; otherwise validation error.• This ensures proper Color material deduction and reporting.
Closing Day/Week:• Close Day saves all attendance, balances and locks the day (no changes without special permission).• Close Week (Thursday) aggregates weekly totals per worker and supports weekly payouts.• Print/Export: all section tables together or per section, for chosen day/week.
9. Composition Manager & Ball MillsPurpose: Define and run Ball Mills for Clay, Glaze and Color, consuming raw materials and producing processed stocks with cost and time tracking.
Ball Mill definitions:• Fields: Name, Type (Clay/Glaze/Color), Capacity, Description, Status.• Each Ball Mill can have linked compositions (formulas).
Composition (per Ball Mill Type):• Product / Material Name (Clay Mix A, Glaze A, Color A, etc.).• Components: list of Raw Materials with quantity per batch.• Yield percentage and expected loss.• Active/Inactive flag.
Running a Ball Mill process:• User selects Ball Mill, Composition and Batch Size.• On 'Start Process': system checks available raw material stock and deducts required quantities immediately.• Process start time and date are recorded; status shown as 'In Process' with blinking indicator.• On 'Finish Process': user enters actual output (kg/liter) and any observed loss;  - Processed stock (Clay/Glaze/Color) is increased in processedStock collection with cost per unit derived from consumed materials.  - Time taken (duration) is stored for analysis.
10. Production TabPurpose: Track production stages, WIP, losses, and movement from processed stock to finished goods.
Production follows Section Groups:• Clay Group → Glaze & Color Group → Kiln Group → Flower Group → Sticker Group → Packing Group.• Each group may have multiple Sub Sections; some Sub Sections are Non Material (labor only).
Link with Workers Activity:• When workers in a material-consuming Sub Section record Qty Worked for a model, system:  - Calculates material required = Qty × Gross Weight/Glaze/Color or per-sheet logic for Flower/Sticker/Packing.  - Deducts from processed stock (Clay/Glaze/Color) and from raw materials for Flower/Sticker/Packing.  - Records any declared loss and updates WIP vs final production.
Final stage – Packing:• When Packing Sub Section completes items for a model, system:  - Converts WIP units to Finished Goods stock for that model, according to Size/Unit.  - Updates production batch, linking all stages and losses to a productionBatch record.  - Costing system uses materials and labour from all stages plus overheads to compute cost per model.
11. Daily Cash RegisterPurpose: Central financial transactions hub: Sales, Purchases, Returns, Cash & Bank transactions and daily summary.
11.1 Sales & InvoicingFields (header):• Customer (dropdown from Customers).• Date.• Document Type: Sales Invoice or Sales Return.• Invoice No. (auto).• Remarks.
Line items:• Item Type: Finished Good / Raw Material.• Item: dropdown filtered by Item Type.• Color (for finished goods, if needed).• Qty.• Unit.• Rate.• Amount (Qty × Rate).• Discount (optional).• Net Amount.
Footer:• Gross Total, Discount Total, Net Total.• Payment Mode: Cash / Bank / Online / Credit / Mixed.• If Cash: amount + cash account.• If Bank/Online: bank account, reference, cheque details.• If Mixed: split between Cash and Bank.• Balance to Customer if partial payment.
Effects:• Sales Invoice:  - Deduct stock from Finished Goods or Raw Materials.  - Increase customer receivable if on credit.  - Update Cash/Bank balances.• Sales Return:  - Increase stock (return units).  - Reduce customer balance.  - Adjust Cash/Bank if refund given.
11.2 Purchase & PaymentsSimilar structure for suppliers:• Supplier, Date, Document Type: Purchase Invoice or Purchase Return.• Lines: Raw Materials and/or other items, Qty, Rate, Amount.• Payment Terms, Due Date.• Payment Mode (Cash/Bank/Online).
Effects:• Purchase Invoice – increase raw material stock, increase supplier payable, adjust Cash/Bank if paid.• Purchase Return – reduce raw material stock, reduce payable, adjust Cash/Bank if refund received.
11.3 Cash Transactions• Used for non-supplier expenses/income (e.g. refreshments, small repairs, sundry income).• Fields: Date, Type (Income/Expense), Category (with Type: Production Overhead/Admin/Selling/Finance), Amount, Description.• Effects: Cash balance and overhead categories.
11.4 Bank Transactions• Used for bank transfers, charges, direct payments.• Fields: Date, Bank Account, Type (Deposit/Withdrawal), Amount, Reference, Description.• Cheque movement handled in Cash Bank tab.• Effects: bank balances.
11.5 Daily Summary• Auto-calculated metrics: Total Sales, Total Sales Returns, Net Sales, Total Purchases, Purchase Returns, Net Purchases, Cash In/Out, Bank In/Out, Net Position, Receivables, Payables.• Printable / exportable with the standard header/footer.
12. Cash Bank TabPurpose: View and manage Cash and Bank balances, fund transfers and cheque status.
Features:• List of Cash and Bank accounts (Account Name, Type, Balance, Last Transaction, Status).• Add/Edit Bank Accounts.• Transfer between Cash and Bank or between Banks.• Cheque Movement: record issued and received cheques, their status (Issued, Cleared, Bounced, Cancelled).
13. Contacts & Directory TabCentral phone directory storing important contacts (customers, suppliers, workers, others) in one place.
Fields: Name, Type (Customer/Supplier/Worker/Other), Cell Number, Email, Last Contact Date, Status, Notes.
Quick actions: Call, WhatsApp, Email, open related master record (if linked).

16. Overheads & CostingOverheads recorded via Cash Register and Bank Transactions with categories and Types (Production Overhead, Admin, Selling, Finance).
Monthly Income & Expenditure Report:• Inputs: period.• Outputs: total Income (Net Sales, other income), Material Purchases (net), Production Overheads, Admin, Selling, Finance, Net Profit.
Model Cost Sheet (Cost of Production per Model):• Direct Material per piece: from Ball Mill compositions and Finished Goods Gross Weight/Glaze/Color, plus Flower/Sticker/Packing usage.• Direct Labour per piece: from Workers Activity for that model.• Production Overhead per piece: Total Production Overhead / Total finished units (all models) or advanced allocation logic later.• Cost Price per piece = Material + Labour + Overhead.• Report shows breakdown and compares with Selling Price.

Reports & AuditKey reports:• Stock reports (Raw Materials by type, Finished Goods by category/color).• Supplier and Customer ledgers and aging.• Worker ledger and wages summary.• Production and loss reports.• Model cost sheet.• Monthly Income & Expenditure.• Trial and license/usage reports for Super Admin.
Audit logs:• For critical entities (users, permissions, opening balances, deletions), store: who, what, when, old and new values.• Dashboard shows daily log summary for Admin/Super Admin.
